Tips for Reducing Stress
  • Exercise:
    • Regular physical activity can help relieve stress. Try to exercise at least 20 minutes, three times a week.
  • sleep:
    • Aim to get seven to nine hours of sleep each night. Try to establish a regular bedtime schedule, and avoid using electronics before bed.
  • Eat well:
    • Eat a nutritious diet with lots of fresh fruits and vegetables.
  • Breaks:
    • Make time to relax and recharge. You can try yoga, meditation, prayer, or muscle relaxation.
  • Mindfulness:
    • Mindfulness meditation can help reduce stress and anxiety.
  • Connect with others:
    • Spend time with people who make you feel good about yourself. Having a strong social support network can help buffer against stress.
  • Smarter:
    • Prioritize your work and focus on tasks that make a difference.
  • Journal:
    • Writing down your worries can help you challenge them and look at things more flexibly.
  • Aware of your habits:
    • Avoid smoking and drinking, which can often make problems worse.
  • Take time to have fun:
    • Make time to enjoy your life and balance your responsibilities.
