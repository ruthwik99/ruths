Tips for Reducing Sadness
  • Exercise:
    Regular exercise can improve your mood and increase your self-confidence.
  • Socialize:
    Spend time with friends and family, or try to meet new people through exercise.
  • Eat well:
    Try to eat healthy, well-balanced meals regularly.
  • Sleep well:
    Stick to a regular sleep schedule.
  • Avoid substances:
    Avoid alcohol and drugs, which can make depression worse.
  • Break tasks into smaller ones:
    Set priorities and do what you can when you can.
  • Do something nice:
    Try doing something nice for someone else.
  • Be patient:
    Expect your mood to improve slowly, not right away.
  • Try alternative therapies:
    Complementary and alternative medicine (CAM) can include acupuncture, aromatherapy, meditation, and yoga.
