Tips For Reducing Anxiety
  • Deep Breathing: Try slow, controlled breathing exercises.
  • Mindfulness/Meditation: Focus on the present moment to calm your mind.
  • Exercise: Regular physical activity can lower stress.
  • Limit Stimulants: Cut back on caffeine or nicotine.
  • Good Sleep: Ensure 7-9 hours of quality rest.
  • Journaling: Write down your thoughts to process emotions.
  • Cognitive Techniques: Challenge negative thoughts and reframe them.
  • Talk to Someone: Share your feelings with a friend or therapist.
  • Relaxation: Use techniques like progressive muscle relaxation.
